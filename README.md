Hi, my name is Maxime and I am engineering student in highschool.
This is a project for Stardance by Hackclub, I am fascinated by drones and things that fly in general so I decided to make a drone for stardance.

I want my project to have some strict criteria:
1. The drone is fixed-wing.
2. Most/all structural parts are 3d-printable on small printers (180mmx180mmx180mm).
3. The cost of the drone is as low as possible.

These criteria are the baseline, in future versions I want to add the ability to take the carbon rod out to make transport easier.
